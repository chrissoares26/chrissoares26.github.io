Saratoga Springs

Life in Saratoga Springs

Since its incorporation in December 1997, Saratoga Springs has rapidly grown from a population of barely 1,000 to a population that exceeds 25,000 today. Our residents love it here because of the easy accessibility to great recreation, the breathtaking views of Utah Lake and the Wasatch front, and the ability to get away from it all. Life really is Just Better Here!

Community Events

The Saratoga Springs Civic Events consists of 6 committees of volunteers including the Arts Council, Veterans Advisory Council, Youth Council, Senior Council, City Celebrations Committee, and Miss Saratoga Springs Pageant Committee. The Civic Events Coordinators work with these volunteer committees to provide resources, events and programs to enrich the lives of citizens and build community in Saratoga Springs.
