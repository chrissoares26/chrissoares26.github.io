Target Audience

Who is this site for? Who will want to use this site?

The site will be for everyone that wants to know the weather forecast in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Who?

People who want check the weather for trips, daily knowledge and commutes

Age Group

Ages 15+ will use the site

Income

There is no income range specification.

Education

The minimum education required is to know how to read.

Personas

Persona 1:

Jordan Clark

-Jordan is 57 years old. He is retired, and he loves to do outdoor activities with his wife and grandkids.

Persona 2:

Petre Carr

-Peter is married and live with his wife and 3 kids. He checks the weather everyday because he ride his bike to go to work.
