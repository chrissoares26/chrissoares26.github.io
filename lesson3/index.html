Weather Site Domain Name

The domain name is "The Weather Guy, www.theweatherguy.org". Currently this domain name is available from source Google Domains for $12/year.

Weather Site Purpose

The Weather Guy website is here to help residents of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven to check local weather. There will be a Storm Center page for viewers to get the latest news related to storms in the area. Each city will have its page, and this page will have the current weather and information for that specific location.
